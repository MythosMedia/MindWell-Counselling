About Me
Ainsley

Ainsley is a Registered Clinical Counsellor who completed her Master of Education (M.Ed.) in Counselling Psychology degree from the University of British Columbia. She also holds a B.A. in Psychology with a minor in Counselling and Human Development from Simon Fraser University. Ainsley uses a person-centered approach and emphasizes the importance of creating a safe, collaborative, and empowering therapeutic space.
